Malcolm "Ronin" Wallace

Malcolm "The Ronin" Wallace is a 23-year-old amateur mixed martial artist, competing in the Welterweight division. He trains under his father and also coaches. Wallace began his amateur MMA career in May 2022 with a first-round TKO victory over Austin Tanner at Combat Quest 19. He followed this with a unanimous decision win against Mateo Estrada at Combat Quest 20. In June 2024 Wallace secured a unanimous decision win against Chase Kuria at Combat Night: St. Petersburg.

Accolades

  • Combat Night Welterweight Champion
  • 2x Vigilant MMA Welterweight Champion
  • Vigilant MMA Kickboxing Champion
  • CFFC Welterweight Contender
