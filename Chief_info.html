Marcellus "Master Chief" Wallace

Marcellus "Master Chief" Wallace is a dedicated mixed martial artist competing in the lightweight division. He begun his professional MMA career in 2022, securing a victory against Devin Dorsey via D'arce Choke at Global Legion FC 21. Prior to turning professional, Wallace achieved notable success in the amateur ranks, including becoming the 170lb Welterweight Champion of Vigilant MMA. He trains under the guidance of Coach Crafton Wallace, and continues to refine his skills and pursue his MMA career.

Accolades

  • Former ISKA FL State Light-Middleweight Champion
  • Former ISKA FL SE Regional Middleweight Champion
  • Dirty Boxing Contender
  • Vigilant MMA Welterweight Champion
